WHY TO JOIN "SPORTS" CLUB??

A sports club is a group of people formed for the purpose of playing sports which focuses on promoting and developing interest in a particular sport or particular activity.
Our club focuse may be recreational,instruction,competitive, or a combination of these types of activities based on its rules & regulations.
Our club are strictly voluntary and involvment in sport and enhance the athlete's sports experience and contribute to the athletes overall physical and wellness.
All programs are based on athletes interest and designed to enchance the atheletes experience by creating an enviornment where students can unite in diverse groups to achieve common goals and objectives while encouraging healthy lifestyles.
Sports club provide a valuable learning experience through athletes involvment in fundraising,public,relations,organization and leadership development.
The clubs with the most success and stability are those with active members and dedicated officers.

Responsibilities


Follow the Sports Club rules & regulations.

Participate in Sports club meeting at least once per month.

Try to maintain sportsman spirit throghout the game.

Try to participate in the sport chosen by you.

Review and approve clubs seeking new admittance and/or reinstatement into the program.
